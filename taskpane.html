Markdown Doctor

Convert Markdown to Word format

How to use:

  1. Highlight the markdown text in your document
  2. Click the "Convert Markdown" button below
  3. The selected text will be converted to Word format

Supported Markdown:

  • Bold: **text** or __text__
  • Italic: *text* or _text_
  • Bold Italic: ***text***
  • Headers: # H1, ## H2, ### H3, etc.
  • Lists: - item or 1. item
  • Links: [text](url)
  • Code: `code` or ```code block```
  • Blockquotes: > quote
